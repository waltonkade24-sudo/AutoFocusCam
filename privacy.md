# Privacy Policy

AutoFocusCam does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The app uses the device camera solely to capture photos at the user’s request. All photos are processed and stored locally on the user’s device. AutoFocusCam does not transmit images, video, or metadata to external servers.

AutoFocusCam does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party advertising services.

If you have questions, contact:
support@autofocuscam.app
